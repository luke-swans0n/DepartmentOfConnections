What We Do

The Department of Connections is a messaging service for those who are stuck in the Cook County Jail. Through our service, inmates are able to send SMS messages to friends and loved ones.

Phone calls from Cook County jail do not allow inmates to leave messages. Relying on the 30-second window of a phone ringing to communicate with the outside is both stressful for the caller and the recipient.
